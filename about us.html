ABOUT US

VENUS BEAUTY

With the ambition of becoming the nation's foremost cosmetics leader, Venus Beauty draws upon 19 years of industry expertise and a vision to deliver unparalleled skincare and beauty products to a valued clientele. At Venus Beauty, a profound respect and concern for consumers' well-being have been pivotal principles, guiding the company towards success. This commitment remains unwavering, as Venus Beauty continues to strive for excellence in serving the Sri Lankan market. Under the leadership of Vinethma Kodithuwakku, seasoned professionals with extensive industry knowledge, Venus Beauty prioritizes integrity and professionalism above all. Venus Beauty remains dedicated to advancing the success narrative of the Sri Lankan cosmetics industry, aiming to reach new heights in personal beauty care and lifestyle enhancement.
